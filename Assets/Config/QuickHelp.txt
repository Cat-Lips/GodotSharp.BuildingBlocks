Synopsis:
[ul]
Basic capture the flag driving game
[/ul]

Gameplay:
[ul]
Press escape to toggle between menu mode and play mode
-
Menu Mode:  Use mouse to select a vehicle to drive (press ctrl to bounce if stuck)
Menu Mode:  Press escape to return to free-look mode (hold shift to move faster)
SinglePlayer:  Free range driving/vehicle editor (experimental)
Multiplayer:  Capture the flag (main game)
[/ul]

CTF Scoring:
[ul]
1 point for first capture
2 points for delivery
(currently, no score limit, but try playing to 3 or 10)
[/ul]
